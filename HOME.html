How does it Provide Security?

Unbreakable Security — As Safe As Nuclear Codes

We take your privacy and data security seriously—so seriously, in fact, that we protect your digital time capsules with encryption protocols on par with the security used to safeguard top-level national secrets.

Your memories are locked with advanced end-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication, and time-locked access. Once you set the delivery date, not even we can open it early. Think of it as your own vault—one that even a hacker armed with nuclear codes couldn’t crack.

Your data is your legacy—ours is to keep it safe.
